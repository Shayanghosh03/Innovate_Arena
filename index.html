Our Games

PUBG Mobile

PUBG is a thrilling battle royale game where 100 players parachute onto an island, scavenge for weapons, and fight to be the last one standing. With realistic graphics, intense gameplay, and multiple maps, PUBG offers solo, duo, and squad modes for a heart-racing survival experience.

Free Fire Max

Free Fire is a fast-paced battle royale game where 50 players land on an island and fight for survival in 10-minute matches. With diverse characters, unique abilities, and intense gameplay, it’s all about surviving till the end.

Info

Candy Crush Saga

Candy Crush Saga is a fun and addictive puzzle game where players match colorful candies to complete levels and achieve high scores. With hundreds of levels and exciting challenges, it’s a perfect mix of strategy and sweet satisfaction!

Info

Bright Memory: Infinite

Bright Memory: Infinite is a fast-paced first-person shooter that blends action, shooting, and swordplay in a futuristic world.

Info

Shadow Fight 4: Arena

Shadow Fight 4: Arena is an intense multiplayer fighting game where players control legendary shadow warriors with unique abilities. Engage in real-time 1v1 battles or team-based matches, using precise moves, combos, and strategies to defeat opponents.

Info

Clash of Clans

Clash of Clans is a strategic multiplayer game where players build villages, train armies, and battle against others to defend their clan and conquer enemy bases. Join clans, participate in wars, and upgrade your defenses to dominate the battlefield.

Info

Asphalt 9: Legends

Asphalt 9: Legends is a high-speed arcade racing game featuring hyper-realistic graphics, iconic supercars, and breathtaking tracks. Players can perform incredible stunts, compete in multiplayer races, and climb the leaderboards to become a racing legend.

Info

Angry Birds

Angry Birds is a fun and addictive physics-based game where players launch birds with unique abilities to destroy the pigs' defenses and reclaim their stolen eggs. With challenging levels and creative puzzles, it’s a perfect mix of strategy and fun!

Info

Call of Duty: Mobile

Call of Duty is a legendary first-person shooter franchise known for its intense combat, realistic graphics, and gripping storylines. Players engage in multiplayer battles, war campaigns, and battle royale modes across various historical and modern settings.

Info

Clash Royale

Clash Royale is a fast-paced strategy game where players collect cards, build decks, and battle in real-time duels to destroy enemy towers. With iconic characters from Clash of Clans and exciting multiplayer action, it’s a perfect blend of strategy and fun.

Info

8 Ball Pool

8 Ball Pool is a two-dimensional pool simulator game, with two players facing each other in a number of online modes. Players gain experience and level-up the more they play, although this is not necessarily a measure of skill.
