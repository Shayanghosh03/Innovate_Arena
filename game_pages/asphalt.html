About This Game

Asphalt 9 features licensed supercars, dramatic tracks, and nitro-based racing where precision drifts and route choices are key to winning.

How To Play This Game

  1. Master nitro timing for speed boosts on straights.
  2. Drift through corners to refill nitro.
  3. Use ramps strategically for barrel rolls and faster routes.
  4. Upgrade top-performing cars first.
  5. Learn each map shortcut in career mode.

Perfect nitro usage can win races even with lower-rated cars.
