About This Game

Call of Duty: Mobile offers multiple game modes, iconic maps, and deep progression where map control and communication matter as much as aim.

How To Play This Game

  1. Customize loadouts for different map ranges.
  2. Use minimap and audio to predict enemy movement.
  3. Play objective first in domination and hardpoint modes.
  4. Use scorestreaks at high-impact moments.
  5. Practice recoil control and movement in training.

Strong positioning and crosshair placement win most gunfights.
