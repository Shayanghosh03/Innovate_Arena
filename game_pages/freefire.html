About This Game

Free Fire Max focuses on short and intense matches where players must loot quickly and push smart fights to survive the final circle.

How To Play This Game

  1. Choose a landing spot with moderate loot and low risk.
  2. Collect a short-range and long-range weapon combo.
  3. Use gloo walls and movement abilities to avoid damage.
  4. Stay close to cover while rotating into safe zones.
  5. Coordinate with your squad for revives and pushes.

Quick decision making matters more than perfect aim in close circles.
