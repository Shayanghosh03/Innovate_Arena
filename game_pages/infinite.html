About This Game

Bright Memory: Infinite rewards aggressive combat by encouraging players to chain ranged and melee attacks while dodging enemy patterns.

Bright Memory: Infinite is a fast-paced action shooter that combines gunplay with sword combat and special abilities. The game follows a futuristic storyline where players control a character named Shelia, fighting enemies using both modern weapons and supernatural powers. It is known for its high-quality graphics, smooth gameplay, and intense combat sequences, making it popular among players who enjoy visually rich and action-packed games.

How To Play This Game

  1. Learn enemy attack timing and dodge early.
  2. Chain sword strikes after ranged shots for combos.
  3. Use skills to control crowds in close arenas.
  4. Prioritize dangerous enemies first.
  5. Upgrade abilities to match your play style.

The best results come from combining movement, parry timing, and weapon swaps.
