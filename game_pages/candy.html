Candy Crush Saga

Swap colorful candies, make combos, and clear objectives in this classic puzzle game loved by all ages.

Candy Crush belongs to the “match-three” genre, where players swap adjacent candies on a board to form a row or column of three or more candies of the same color. When matched, the candies disappear and new ones fall into place, often creating chain reactions that increase the score. The game is divided into thousands of levels, each with different objectives such as clearing jelly, reaching a target score, or bringing special ingredients to the bottom of the board within a limited number of moves. Matching more than three candies creates special candies with powerful effects, making the gameplay more strategic and engaging.

About This Game

Candy Crush Saga challenges players to complete level goals with limited moves. Power candies and cascades help clear difficult boards.

How To Play This Game

  1. Match at least three candies of the same color.
  2. Create special candies by matching four or more.
  3. Focus first on level goals, not just high score.
  4. Use boosters only when a level gets difficult.
  5. Plan moves from bottom to trigger cascades.

Combining two special candies creates very powerful board clears.
