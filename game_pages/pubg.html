About This Game

PUBG Mobile drops players into large maps where they loot weapons, armor, and utilities while the safe zone gets smaller over time. Teamwork and map awareness are essential to win.

How To Play This Game

  1. Land in a safe zone with enough loot spots nearby.
  2. Collect weapons, ammo, armor, and healing items quickly.
  3. Move with the shrinking circle and avoid open fields.
  4. Use sound cues and cover before engaging enemies.
  5. In end game, maintain high ground and position for the final fight.

Practice aim and movement in training mode to improve faster.
