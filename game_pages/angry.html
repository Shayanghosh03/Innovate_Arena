About This Game

Angry Birds is built around trajectory and timing. Each bird has unique skills, and every level rewards smart planning over random shots.

How To Play This Game

  1. Drag the slingshot to choose launch angle and power.
  2. Target weak points in pig structures.
  3. Use each bird's special ability at the right time.
  4. Save birds by finishing with fewer shots.
  5. Retry levels to improve your score.

Observe structure balance before shooting for easier chain reactions.
